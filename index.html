How to use
  1. Install AnkiNBT to your modpack.
  2. Go to the Feeding Menu.
    [reference pic]
  3. Press "N" on the Mounts.
  4. Go to Misc > Copy NBT.
    [reference pic]
  5. Paste the NBT into the box below.
    [reference pic]
  6. Press Submit and all the stats will be inputted.
